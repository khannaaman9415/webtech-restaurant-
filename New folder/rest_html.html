OUR CHEF
He is one of the most recognized faces of Indian television. He is in people's living rooms for almost two decades introducing them to the delights of Indian and international cuisine in his inimitable easy going and cheery manner. The show Khana Khazana was one of those trailblazing cookery Shows on Zee Network. Little wonder then that he has a massive fan following across all age groups, genders and countries.

Passion for food has driven him to every nook and corner of the world. From becoming the youngest executive chef at a five star hotel, to hosting his own cooking shows, it is his love for cooking that has got this far.
